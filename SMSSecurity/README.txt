-------------------------------------

     SMS Sec App Exercise
     
-------------------------------------

           Overview
           
The SMS Sec App is designed to allow
you to remotely control your own pho
ne via SMS. The app introduces a num
ber of concepts:

1. Broadcast receivers
2. SMS reception and notification hi
   ding
3. Shared preferences
4. Shared preference GUI definition
   in XML
5. AlarmManager and scheduled inten
   t broadcasts
6. Capturing images from the camera
   without user interaction
7. Custom listeners on shared prefe
   rences GUI elements
8. Creating a background service


-------------------------------------

       Exercise Steps
       
Steps 1-4 are described in the 
SMSReceiver class.

Step 5 is described in the 
CommandService class.

Step 6. is described in the
CommandManager class.
